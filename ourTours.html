NW Adventures Quest is a not your average bus tour company! We offer a wide range of tours up and down the coast and along the Valley.

Coastal Tours

$35 per passenger

See some of the most beautiful spots on the Oregon Coast! Here are some of the tour stops we make, Sea Lion Caves, Heceta Lighthouse, Cooks Chasm, Thor's Well and Cape Perpetua to name a few. Tours come with complimentary water and healthy snacks.
Winery Tours

$45-$65 per passenger

Take advantage and visit some of the best wineries Oregon has to offer! We have a build your own Winery Tour with up to five winery destinations of your choosing. Or you can let us take you on our standard Winery Tour (2-5 stops) Complimentary water and healthy snacks provided.
Brewery Tours

$35-$45 per passenger

Take your pick of the north or south Brewery Tour which starts in the beautiful coastal town of Florence. Here are some of the Breweries we take you to. Yachats Brewery, Rogue Brewery, 7 Devils Brewery and many more!
Private Events

$300 Half Day (6 HR) $450 Full Day (9 HR)

If you can dream it, we can do it!!! Weddings, Birthdays, Private Group Transportation, Hiking Trips, Biking Trips, Kayak Trips, Conventions, and so much more!!!!
Educational Tours

Groups 6 or Larger Prices will vary

Join us on several Educational Tours on the Oregon Coast with our team of marine biologists and naturalists. We offer Geological Safari's, Oregon Coast Marine Mammal and Tide Pool Exploration, Lighthouse History on the Oregon Coast, Photography Safari's and Much More!!! You can also choose from varying activities to meet your physical capabilities on any of our Educational Tours.
